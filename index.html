BMI Checker


BMI is widely used as a simple method for estimating body fat and assessing weight categories (underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity) in adults. However, it should be noted that BMI does not directly measure body fat and may not be a perfect indicator for every individual due to factors like muscle mass, age, and sex.
